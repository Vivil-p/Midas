Leading Tread Rubber, Precured Tread Rubber, Retreading Material Suppliers since 1969

George Varughese founded General Rubbers, with its Midas brand of retreading materials, in 1969. Since then Midas has been supplying Tread Rubber, Precured Tread Rubber and other retreading materials to India and the rest of the world. Midas is now India's most popular brand of Tyre Retreading Materials with more than 24000 tons sold every year. Besides India, Midas currently has satisfied customers all over the world from South America to Africa and from Europe to Australia.
